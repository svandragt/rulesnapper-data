3. IP policy
It is strictly forbidden to upload counterfeit items to our platform. Counterfeit items usually copy or imitate the trademark (e.g. name of the brand, name of its product and/or logo) of a company without its permission. They may also copy the specific notable features of that company’s product (e.g. the shape of a perfume bottle, the design and specific pattern of a bag). Note: Sale of counterfeit items could expose sellers to civil and criminal proceedings.
Members can only upload items which they are sure are authentic. They cannot add a disclaimer to their listing saying they are not sure whether or not the item is authentic.
Members must also upload
pictures showcasing
as clearly as possible their item’s authenticity.
Using other brands which are not related to your item in the brand category, title/description or as a hashtag in your listing is not allowed.
The use of words such as "inspired by" followed by a brand is also generally prohibited on our platform.
It is strictly prohibited to upload and sell items which are reproduced without IP rights holder’s consent (e.g. copied books, any kind of sport, diet plans created for individual purposes, copied video games into blank CD or in any other kind of medium).

You need to upload each of your items separately, under their corresponding category (and brand, if applicable).
You should only have one listing per item.
All items should have an

You can choose up to 3 materials when uploading an item. A detailed description should also include information about additional materials (besides the main 3 already selected when uploading).

3. IP policy
It is strictly forbidden to upload counterfeit items to our platform. Counterfeit items usually copy or imitate the trademark (e.g. name of the brand, name of its product and/or logo) of a company without its permission. They may also copy the specific notable features of that company’s product (e.g. the shape of a perfume bottle, the design and specific pattern of a bag).
Note:
Sale of counterfeit items could expose sellers to civil and criminal proceedings.
Members can only upload items which they are sure are authentic. They cannot add a disclaimer to their listing saying they are not sure whether or not the item is authentic.
Members must also upload
pictures showcasing
as clearly as possible their item’s authenticity.
Using other brands which are not related to your item in the brand category, title/description or as a hashtag in your listing is not allowed.
The use of words such as "inspired by" followed by a brand is also generally prohibited on our platform.
It is strictly prohibited to upload and sell items which are reproduced without IP rights holder’s consent (e.g. copied books, any kind of sport, diet plans created for individual purposes, copied video games into blank CD or in any other kind of medium).
